Simple and Delicious Aglio Olio

I fell in love with Aglio Olio Pasta during my college days. Let's learn how to make this beautiful dish today

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Red Chilli Flakes
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil 2 litres of water in a big pot.
  2. Add good amount of salt, the water should feel fairly salty. But not like sea water salty.
  3. Put the Sphagetti in the boiling water. The cooking time is written on the packets of most pastas. Kindly check the instructions. Do not overcook the pasta
  4. Peel the garlic cloves.
  5. On a separate pan, put generous amount of olive oil in pan (2-3 tpsn).
  6. Chop the garlic. Add the chopped garlic to the pan.
  7. Cook on low to medium heat. Let the oil be infused with the flavours of the garlic. Don't burn the garlic.
  8. After the garlic is cooked, take some pasta water from pot and put in the pot to arrest the cooking.
  9. Add the spaghetti in the pan and toss well. Add chilli flakes.
  10. Garnish with parsley and enjoy.
